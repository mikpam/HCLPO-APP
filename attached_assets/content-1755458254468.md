# Gemini 2.5 Pro  bookmark\_border   Stay organized with collections     Save and categorize content based on your preferences.

Release Notes


Gemini 2.5 Pro is our most advanced reasoning Gemini model,
capable of solving complex problems. Gemini 2.5 Pro can comprehend vast
datasets and challenging problems from different information sources, including
text, audio, images, video, and even entire code repositories.

For even more detailed technical information on Gemini 2.5 Pro (such as
performance benchmarks, information on our training datasets, efforts on
sustainability, intended usage and limitations, and our approach to ethics and
safety), see our [technical\\
report](https://storage.googleapis.com/deepmind-media/gemini/gemini_v2_5_report.pdf)
on our Gemini 2.5 models and the [model card for\\
Gemini 2.5 Pro](https://storage.googleapis.com/model-cards/documents/gemini-2.5-pro.pdf).

[Try in Vertex AI](https://console.cloud.google.com/vertex-ai/generative/multimodal/create/text?model=gemini-2.5-pro) [View in Model Garden](https://console.cloud.google.com/vertex-ai/publishers/google/model-garden/gemini-2.5-pro) [(Preview) Deploy example app](https://console.cloud.google.com/vertex-ai/studio/multimodal?suggestedPrompt=How%20does%20AI%20work&deploy=true&model=gemini-2.5-pro)

Note: To use the "Deploy example app" feature, you need a Google Cloud project with billing and Vertex AI API enabled.
